INDUSTRIAL CORE SYSTEM™

Your Fast, Foundational ERP-Alternative System

Built on Zoho. Designed for Industrial SMBs. Ready in Weeks.

The Industrial Core System™ is the fastest way for small and mid-sized manufacturers, distributors, and logistics companies to move off spreadsheets, paper processes, and outdated tools — and onto a modern, connected, cloud-based operating system.

It provides the essential building blocks of an ERP-alternative system without the heavy cost, long timelines, or complexity of traditional ERPs.

This is the ideal starting point after Blueprint™ for companies who need quick results and a scalable foundation.

WHAT IS THE INDUSTRIAL CORE SYSTEM™?

A streamlined, fast-deployment setup of Zoho’s core operational applications:

  • Zoho CRM — Sales → Quote → Order
  • Zoho Inventory — Stock, warehouses, fulfillment
  • Zoho Books — Billing, AP/AR workflows
  • Zoho Flow — Basic cross-department automation
  • Zoho Analytics — Core KPIs & dashboards

Plus foundational elements:

  • Security setup
  • Roles & permissions
  • Data structure configuration
  • Basic automations
  • Light Creator app (optional)
  • Go-Live training & support

It is not a full ERP replacement — instead, it is the fastest path toward operational modernization.

WHO IT’S FOR

The Industrial Core System™ is ideal for companies that:

  • Run operations on Excel, paper, or emails
  • Use Acomba, Sage, or lightweight tools
  • Have no automation between sales, warehouse, and finance
  • Want rapid modernization without major disruption
  • Need early wins before investing in full Industrial OS™

Perfect for:

  • Small manufacturers
  • Distributors
  • Logistics & fleet operators
  • Warehouses
  • Industrial supply operations
